# Welcome to the BrokenScope Content Repository!

Thank you for your interest in shaping the narrative of BrokenScope. Here, you can contribute investigations, subjects, or organizations by simply uploading your content in Markdown format. Every `.md` file you add is dynamically rendered into HTML by the BrokenScope architecture, making your contributions come to life effortlessly on the website.

Before you get started, please review our [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) file to ensure your submissions meet our guidelines and standards.

Happy contributing!
